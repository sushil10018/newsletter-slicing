My Time Special Interest Day Programs

 

Green Army - Wednesday

This term Green Army have raised $18 from their crushed cans and all have voted to buy Westside a new cricket set for all to enjoy. Be-cause of the teamwork by the Green Army, the worms are growing and getting fat and the compost is doing its thing. Our outdoor area is always tidy from leaves and rubbish with a weekly rake and clean up, the guys are working hard at keeping our immediate environment looking tip top.

Participants: Jake, Matt, Mellissa, Sam, Thomas
Facilitator: Meg

 

Girl Power - Thursday

Alana and I are so proud of the caring, happy, helpful, funny wom-en we have in Girl Power and the beautiful friendships they’ve formed here. There’s always so much laughter and encourage-ment in this group. I think I speak for Alana as well when I say how much I enjoy spending time with them, and I hope we’re positive role models for them.

Participants: Kathy, Zoe, Rebecca, Vanessa, Juliet, Courtney, Elysha, Melissa, Laura, Gabby
Facilitator: Bec and Alana

 

Foundation Skills & Work Readiness - Wednesday

This group has continued to increase their responsibility and independence, and improve upon their communication and team work skills, organising a great outing to Visible Ink via Public transport with Green Army, completing a shopping and cooking challenge, presenting to the group and practicing the basic of interviewing! I’m very proud of their progress and their small wins, and especially their work on their newsletter!

Participants: Caleb, Connor, JR, Nathan, Jace
Facilitator: Bec

Our Favorite Healthy Recipie

Ingredients

1 Large Brushed Potato
Topping Ideas

  • Ham, cheese & pineapple
  • Bacon & sour cream
  • Tuna, corn & mayo
  • Taco mince & guacamole
  • Baked beans

Method

  • Wash the skin of the potato, then dry with a paper towel
  • Prick several times using a fork
  • Place the potato on a microwave-safe plate
  • Cook on full power for 5 minutes
  • Turnover and continue to cook for about 4-5 minutes more
  • When the potato is soft remove from the microwave and cut in quarters
